Safety
  • Cats must be presented to the Driver in an enclosed Cat Carrier.
  • Dogs must be handed over wearing a body harness & leash.
  • All Drivers have seat belt adapters that Carriers and Harnesses clip into.
  • This service is for unaccompanied pets to get to pre-booked appointments or locations. We are not licensed to transport Humans with their pets.
  • This is not an animal ambulance service. If a pet is ill, please phone an Animal Ambulance or a Mobile Vet.
  • Please ensure Grooming & Vet Appointments have been pre-booked before scheduling a Pet Taxi ride.
  • If a pet vomits or toilets, a cleaning fee of $100 is payable by the Pet Owner.
  • No Aggressive Dogs.
  • Posting a Job for a Pet Taxi is only confirmed once an available Driver accepts the Booking which notifies you and you fund your PetCloud wallet with adequate credits for the trip.
What's Included?
  • Booking Admin fee
  • Public liability & Pet Transit Insurance
  • Trained, Police checked, Australian licensed Pet Taxi Driver.
Additional Charges, only if applicable
  • Toll Fees: actual cost incurred.
  • Cancellation Fee: $10.
  • Extra waiting charges: $1 per min. Starts after 5 mins of waiting.
  • Pet Mess Cleaning fee $100.
