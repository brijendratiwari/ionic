Listing Photos Photo Upload Note

Add 1 to 5 photos for your listing. The first image will be your hero banner so this should be a long wide image.

The photos you display are important as they tell a Pet Owner about you and your home, yard & services. Pet Owners will want to see where their pet will safely sleep, play, be bathed, groomed or walked.

Photo suggestions
  • Ensure they are clear quality photos with good lighting
  • A photo of your yard & fence (where the pet might play)
  • A photo of your Lounge Area (where the pet might sleep)
  • A photo of the Local Dog Park or Foot Path
  • An Action photo of a pet being walked, fed, washed
